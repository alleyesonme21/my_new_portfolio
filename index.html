About Me

Originally from Haiti, I moved to the United States in 2018. I am a former international development worker (Doctors Without Borders) and professional touring reggae guitarist. I am passionate about development in my native Haiti, music and the joy that it can bring, and computer programming. I am proudly tri-lingual and speak Haitian Creole, French and English fluently.

Professional

My decision to learn programming at Epicodus is informed by my desire to learn in a collaborative and intensive environment and have practical work force exposure through the hand's on internship program. My short term goal is work for a tech company focused on music, arts and education. My long-term goal is to build a programming school in Haiti and provide job skills training in my country.
